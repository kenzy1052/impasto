Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our photography services, packages, and booking process. Can't find what you're looking for? Feel free to contact us directly.

Welcome to Impasto Photography

At Impasto Photography, we understand that choosing the right photographer for your special moments is an important decision. We've compiled answers to the most common questions to help you understand our services, process, and what makes us unique.

Our team is dedicated to providing exceptional photography experiences that capture your story with artistry and professionalism. Whether you're planning a wedding, portrait session, or special event, we're here to help make your vision come to life.

How do I book a photography session?

Booking with Impasto Photography is simple and straightforward:

  • Contact us through our website, phone, or email
  • Schedule a consultation to discuss your needs and vision
  • Review and select your preferred package
  • Sign the contract and pay the booking deposit
  • Receive confirmation with session details

We recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance for weddings and 1-2 weeks for portrait sessions to ensure availability.

What photography services do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of photography services:

  • Wedding Photography & Videography
  • Pre-wedding & Engagement Sessions
  • Portrait Photography (Individual, Family, Corporate)
  • Event Coverage & Documentation
  • Birthday & Celebration Photography
  • Product & Commercial Photography
  • Passport Photography
  • Large Format Printing Services

What are your photography packages and pricing?

Our packages are designed to meet different needs and budgets:

  • Pre/Post Wedding: ₵2,500 - Includes 2-hour session and 50+ edited images
  • Same Day Wedding: ₵4,500 - 8 hours coverage with 200+ images
  • Love Chronicles (2-day): ₵7,500 - Traditional and white wedding coverage
  • Signature Experience (3-day): ₵12,000 - Complete wedding documentation
  • Combined Photo + Video: ₵7,500 - ₵15,000 depending on package

All packages include professional editing, online gallery, and print release. Custom packages available upon request.

What is your payment structure?

Our payment structure is designed for your convenience:

  • Booking Deposit: 50% of total package cost to secure your date
  • Final Payment: Remaining 50% due 7 days before your session/event
  • Payment Methods: Bank transfer, mobile money, or cash
  • Additional Services: Can be paid for separately or added to final payment

Payment plans can be arranged for larger packages. Contact us to discuss flexible payment options.

What is your cancellation and rescheduling policy?

We understand that plans can change. Here's our policy:

  • Rescheduling: Free rescheduling up to 14 days before your session
  • Weather Delays: Outdoor sessions can be rescheduled due to weather at no charge
  • Cancellation (30+ days): 75% refund of deposit
  • Cancellation (14-30 days): 50% refund of deposit
  • Cancellation (Less than 14 days): Non-refundable

Emergency situations will be considered on a case-by-case basis.

How long does a typical photography session last?

Session duration depends on the type of shoot:

  • Portrait Sessions: 1-2 hours
  • Pre-wedding Shoots: 2-3 hours
  • Same Day Wedding: 8-10 hours
  • Multi-day Weddings: 2-3 days coverage
  • Event Coverage: 4-8 hours depending on event

We work at a comfortable pace to ensure we capture all the important moments without rushing.

When will I receive my photos and how are they delivered?

Our delivery timeline ensures quality while meeting your expectations:

  • Preview/Sneak Peek: 24-48 hours after your session
  • Portrait Sessions: 7-14 days for full gallery
  • Wedding Photography: 3-4 weeks for complete collection
  • Express Service: 5 working days (additional ₵5,000)

Delivery Methods:

  • Online gallery with download links
  • USB drive with high-resolution images
  • Cloud storage sharing (Google Drive/Dropbox)

Do you provide the original/unedited photos?

We provide professionally edited images as our final product. Here's why:

  • Professional editing is part of our artistic process
  • Color correction and exposure adjustments enhance image quality
  • Consistent style and branding across all images
  • Unedited photos may not represent our professional standards

However, if you specifically need unedited files for professional purposes, please discuss this during booking. Additional fees may apply.

Do you travel for photoshoots?

Yes, we are available for travel both locally and internationally. Our standard service area includes:

  • All major cities in Ghana
  • Surrounding regions within a 100km radius of Accra

For locations outside our standard service area, travel fees may apply. These typically include:

  • Transportation costs
  • Accommodation (if required)
  • Per diem for extended assignments

For international assignments, please contact us for a custom quote based on your specific location and requirements.

Do you travel for destination weddings or sessions?

Yes! We love destination photography and are available for travel:

  • Within Accra: No additional travel charges
  • Greater Accra Region: Minimal travel fee may apply
  • Other Regions in Ghana: Travel and accommodation costs additional
  • International: Custom quotes including travel, visa, and accommodation

Destination sessions often create unique and memorable images. Contact us early to discuss travel logistics and costs.

What happens if there's bad weather on my session day?

We have contingency plans for weather-related challenges:

  • Light Rain: We can work with umbrellas and covered areas
  • Heavy Weather: Free rescheduling to the next available date
  • Indoor Alternatives: We can suggest indoor locations if needed
  • Wedding Day Weather: We bring lighting equipment for indoor backup plans

We monitor weather forecasts closely and will communicate with you about any necessary changes 24-48 hours in advance.

Can you accommodate special photography requests or styles?

Absolutely! We love creative challenges and special requests:

  • Themed Sessions: Vintage, traditional, modern, artistic styles
  • Cultural Photography: Traditional ceremonies and cultural elements
  • Special Effects: Smoke, lighting effects, creative compositions
  • Specific Shots: Family photos, group arrangements, special poses
  • Props and Setup: We can arrange special props or backdrops

Discuss your vision during the consultation. Some special requests may incur additional costs, which we'll discuss upfront.

Do you provide raw, unedited photos?

We do not typically provide raw, unedited photos as part of our standard packages. Here's why:

  • Raw files require specialized software and knowledge to process properly
  • Our editing style is an integral part of our artistic vision
  • We ensure all delivered images meet our quality standards

In some cases, we may provide raw files for an additional fee, but this is evaluated on a case-by-case basis. We believe that the final edited images best represent our work and your memories.

Do you offer printing services and photo products?

Yes! We offer a range of professional printing services:

  • Photo Books: A4 (₵1,500) and A3 (₵2,000) premium albums
  • Print Frames: A2 size professional frames (₵600)
  • Large Format Printing: Professional prints up to A0 size
  • Canvas Prints: Gallery-wrapped canvas options available
  • Digital Files: High-resolution files for your own printing needs

All printed products use professional-grade materials and are produced in-house for quality control.

What should I wear for my photography session?

Clothing choices can greatly impact your photos. Here are our recommendations:

  • Solid Colors: Work better than busy patterns
  • Coordinated Outfits: For couples/families, coordinate but don't match exactly
  • Comfort First: Wear something you feel confident and comfortable in
  • Bring Options: 2-3 outfit changes for variety
  • Consider the Location: Dress appropriately for indoor/outdoor settings
  • Avoid: Large logos, neon colors, and very small patterns

We'll provide a detailed styling guide after booking to help you prepare.

Do you have backup equipment and contingency plans?

Professional reliability is our priority. Our backup systems include:

  • Multiple Cameras: Primary and backup camera bodies
  • Lens Collection: Various focal lengths for different situations
  • Memory Cards: Multiple high-capacity, high-speed cards
  • Battery Systems: Extended battery packs and charging solutions
  • Lighting Equipment: Professional flash systems and continuous lights
  • Backup Photographer: Available for important events if needed

Your photos are backed up immediately after shooting and stored in multiple locations.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept various forms of payment for your convenience:

  • Bank transfer
  • Mobile money (MTN, Telecel, AirtelTigo)
  • Cash (in-person only)
  • Credit/debit cards (through our secure online payment system)

For all bookings, a 50% deposit is required to secure your date, with the remaining balance due 7 days before the event date.

Can I see examples of your work before booking?

Absolutely! We encourage you to review our work before making a decision:

  • Portfolio Gallery: Extensive online portfolio on our website
  • Social Media: Recent work showcased on Instagram and Facebook
  • Client Galleries: Sample wedding and portrait galleries available
  • Physical Portfolio: In-person viewing available during consultations
  • Testimonials: Client reviews and feedback available

We're proud of our work and want you to feel confident in your choice.

What makes Impasto Photography different from other studios?

Our unique approach combines artistry with professionalism:

  • Artistic Vision: We create images that tell your story with emotion and beauty
  • Professional Excellence: Years of experience and technical expertise
  • Personal Service: We get to know you to capture your authentic moments
  • Cultural Sensitivity: Understanding and respect for Ghanaian traditions
  • Complete Service: From consultation to final delivery, we handle everything
  • Quality Guarantee: We stand behind our work with professional standards

Our goal is not just to take photos, but to create lasting memories and art.
