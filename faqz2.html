Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our photography services, pricing, and booking process.

What types of photography sessions do you offer?

We offer a variety of photography sessions including:

  • Portrait photography for individuals and couples
  • Family photography sessions
  • Wedding photography coverage
  • Engagement and pre-wedding sessions
  • Maternity photography
  • Business headshots and corporate photography

How much do your photography sessions cost?

Our pricing varies depending on the type of session and duration. Here are our general price ranges:

  • Portrait Sessions: $150 - $400
  • Family Sessions: $250 - $600
  • Weddings: Starting at $1,200
  • Engagement Sessions: $350 - $650
  • Corporate Headshots: $200 - $450 per person

All prices are subject to change and may vary based on location and specific requirements. Contact us for a personalized quote.

Do you travel for sessions?

Yes, we travel throughout the [Your Service Area] region for sessions. Travel fees may apply for locations outside our immediate service area. We recommend contacting us to discuss your specific location and any associated travel costs.

How long does a typical session last?

Session durations vary based on the type of session:

  • Portrait Sessions: 1-2 hours
  • Family Sessions: 1.5-3 hours
  • Weddings: 8-12 hours (full day coverage)
  • Engagement Sessions: 2-4 hours

We always work with clients to ensure they get the coverage they desire without feeling rushed.

When will I receive my photos after the session?

Turnaround times depend on the complexity of the session and our current workload:

  • Portrait & Family Sessions: Typically 2-3 weeks
  • Weddings: Usually 6-8 weeks
  • Express Editing: Available upon request for additional fee

We provide regular updates during the editing process and deliver final images via a secure online gallery.

What is included in your photography packages?

Our standard packages typically include:

  • Photographer time and expertise
  • Professional equipment and backup systems
  • High-quality digital files (unedited and edited versions)
  • Online gallery for viewing and downloading
  • Basic retouching and color correction
  • Print release for personal use

Premium packages may include additional services such as album design, premium printing, or extended coverage.

Can I reschedule or cancel my session?

We understand that plans can change. Our cancellation policy is as follows:

  • Cancellations: Full refund if cancelled 48 hours before scheduled session
  • Rescheduling: Free rescheduling up to 24 hours before session
  • Last-Minute Changes: May incur a small administrative fee

In case of illness or emergency, please contact us immediately and we'll work with you to find a solution.

Do you provide props or wardrobe suggestions?

Yes, we provide guidance on what works best for photography sessions:

  • Wardrobe Tips: Solid colors, simple patterns, and comfortable clothing
  • Props: We can provide basic props or suggest items to bring from home
  • Seasonal Considerations: Advice on appropriate attire for weather conditions

We're happy to review your planned outfits and make recommendations to ensure you look your best in photos.

Do you edit the photos?

Yes, all photos undergo professional retouching including:

  • Color correction and enhancement
  • Skin retouching and blemish removal
  • Exposure and contrast adjustments
  • White balance correction
  • Minor object removal

We maintain a natural look while enhancing your features and ensuring every image meets our quality standards.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience:

  • Cash (for in-person payments)
  • Credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • Bank transfers
  • Digital wallets (PayPal, Venmo)

A non-refundable deposit is required to secure your booking date, with the remaining balance due on or before your session date.
