Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, booking process, and what to expect during your photography session.

How do I book a photography session?

Booking a photography session with us is simple and straightforward. Follow these steps:

  • Contact us through our website or by phone to discuss your requirements
  • We'll schedule a consultation to understand your vision and needs
  • Choose a package that best suits your requirements
  • Pay a 50% deposit to secure your date
  • We'll send a confirmation email with all the details

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for weddings and major events to ensure availability.

What is included in your photography packages?

Our photography packages typically include:

  • Professional photography services for the specified duration
  • High-resolution edited digital images
  • Online gallery for viewing and sharing
  • Print release for personal printing
  • USB drive with all final images

Premium packages may include additional services such as:

  • Second photographer
  • Extended hours
  • Albums or prints
  • Same-day edits
How long does it take to receive the final photos?

The turnaround time for final photos depends on the type of session:

  • Portrait sessions: 2-3 weeks
  • Events: 3-4 weeks
  • Weddings: 6-8 weeks

During peak seasons, these timelines may be slightly extended. We always provide an estimated delivery date at the time of booking and keep you updated throughout the editing process.

Express delivery options are available for an additional fee if you need your photos sooner.

Do you travel for photoshoots?

Yes, we are available for travel both locally and internationally. Our standard service area includes:

  • All major cities in Ghana
  • Surrounding regions within a 100km radius of Accra

For locations outside our standard service area, travel fees may apply. These typically include:

  • Transportation costs
  • Accommodation (if required)
  • Per diem for extended assignments

For international assignments, please contact us for a custom quote based on your specific location and requirements.

What is your cancellation and rescheduling policy?

We understand that sometimes plans change. Our cancellation and rescheduling policy is as follows:

  • Cancellations made more than 90 days before the event: Full refund of deposit
  • Cancellations made 60-90 days before the event: 50% refund of deposit
  • Cancellations made 30-60 days before the event: 25% refund of deposit
  • Cancellations made less than 30 days before the event: No refund of deposit

Rescheduling is subject to availability. We allow one complimentary reschedule if made more than 30 days before the event. Additional reschedules may incur a fee.

How many photos will I receive from my session?

The number of photos you receive depends on the type and duration of your session:

  • Portrait sessions (1 hour): 30-50 edited photos
  • Pre-wedding sessions (2 hours): 50-80 edited photos
  • Wedding day (8 hours): 300-500 edited photos
  • Multi-day events: Proportionally more photos

We focus on quality over quantity, ensuring each image meets our high standards before delivery. We carefully select and edit the best moments from your session to create a collection that tells your story beautifully.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept various forms of payment for your convenience:

  • Bank transfer
  • Mobile money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo)
  • Cash (in-person only)
  • Credit/debit cards (through our secure online payment system)

For all bookings, a 50% deposit is required to secure your date, with the remaining balance due 7 days before the event date.

Do you provide raw, unedited photos?

We do not typically provide raw, unedited photos as part of our standard packages. Here's why:

  • Raw files require specialized software and knowledge to process properly
  • Our editing style is an integral part of our artistic vision
  • We ensure all delivered images meet our quality standards

In some cases, we may provide raw files for an additional fee, but this is evaluated on a case-by-case basis. We believe that the final edited images best represent our work and your memories.
